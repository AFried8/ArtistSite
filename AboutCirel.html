Questions for Cirel

When did you start painting?

I've been doodling with my pen or pencil for as long as I can remember but my formal painting education began when I was nine, and I haven't stopped since

Which is your favorite painting?

That's a hard question! I do enjoy painting scenes from Yerushalayim

Do you ever get artist's block?

It's usually not too bad, but if I do, I'll often take a walk and ideas will come to me then



Cirel Jacobs

Cirel Jacobs is an inspired artist living in New York. Her artwork is displayed in several galleries across the city
